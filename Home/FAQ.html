Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the return policy for accessories?

Accessories can be returned within 7 days of purchase, provided they are in original packaging and unused. Please keep your receipt as proof of purchase.

2. Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes, most repair services come with a limited warranty. The duration of the warranty depends on the type of repair and will be specified at the time of service.

3. How long will it take to repair my device?

Repair times vary depending on the issue and device type. Most common repairs are completed within 1-2 hours. We’ll give you an estimated time when you bring your device in.

4. Do you use original parts for repairs?

We use high-quality, OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) or certified third-party parts based on availability and customer preference. We’ll discuss options with you before starting any repair.

5. Can I track my repair status?

Yes! You can track the status of your repair through our website or contact us directly. We’ll also notify you once the repair is complete.

6. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payment options for in-store purchases and services.

7. Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

We offer seasonal discounts and promotions on selected items and services. Check our website or follow us on social media for the latest updates.
