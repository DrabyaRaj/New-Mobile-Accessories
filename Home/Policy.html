Our Policies

1. Return & Exchange Policy

  • Eligibility: Products can be returned or exchanged within 7 days of purchase if they are in their original condition and packaging.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Customized accessories, opened screen protectors, and certain electronic components are non-returnable.
  • Process: Customers must provide a receipt or proof of purchase for all returns or exchanges.
  • Refund Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method or as store credit.

2. Warranty Policy

  • Coverage: Most accessories and repaired items come with a limited warranty (specific period depends on the product).
  • Exclusions: Warranty does not cover physical damage, water damage, or tampering by unauthorized personnel.
  • Claim Process: Warranty claims require proof of purchase and may involve inspection by our technicians.

3. Privacy Policy

  • Personal Information: We respect customer privacy and do not share personal data with third parties without consent.
  • Data Security: All sensitive information is stored securely to protect customer privacy.
  • Usage: Data is used strictly for order processing, customer support, and personalized offers.
